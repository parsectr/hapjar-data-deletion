Our Data Policy

HappinessJar is designed with privacy at its core. All your memories, photos, and data are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information on our servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Since all data in HappinessJar is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. You can delete your data in the following ways:

Option 1: In-App Data Deletion

The easiest way to delete all your data is directly within the app:

  1. Open the HappinessJar app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the "Danger Zone" section
  4. Tap "Delete All Data"
  5. Confirm your choice

This will permanently delete all memories, photos, and related data from your device.

Option 2: Uninstall the App

You can also remove all app data by uninstalling HappinessJar from your device:

  1. Find HappinessJar on your device's home screen or app drawer
  2. Press and hold the app icon
  3. Select "Uninstall" or drag to the uninstall area
  4. Confirm the uninstallation

This will remove the app and all associated data from your device.
